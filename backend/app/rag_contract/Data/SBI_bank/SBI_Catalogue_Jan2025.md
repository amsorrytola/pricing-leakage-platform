## 1. INTRODUCTION

State Bank of India, the largest public sector bank in India, maintains standardized pricing across all branches. Our pricing is designed to be accessible and transparent, with minimal variation. We serve government entities, PSUs, and private corporate clients with equal terms unless mandated otherwise by regulatory directives.

All fees are in INR, exclusive of 18% GST. Annual escalation is 3.0% per annum (RBI-linked) effective January 1 each year.
