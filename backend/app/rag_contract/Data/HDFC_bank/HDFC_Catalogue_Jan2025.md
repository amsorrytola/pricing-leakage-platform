## 1. PRICING PHILOSOPHY

HDFC Bank maintains competitive pricing reflecting our market leadership position. Our fee structure offers strong volume discounts for high-transaction clients and relationship-based pricing tiers. We focus on transparent pricing with clear volume breakpoints.

All fees are in Indian Rupees (INR), exclusive of 18% GST. Fees are subject to 3.5% annual escalation effective January 1 each year unless otherwise negotiated.
