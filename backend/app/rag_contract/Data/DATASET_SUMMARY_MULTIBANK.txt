    Calculate: Annual revenue loss

  Compare your extracted data to CSV metadata:
    Anomaly type matches?
    Difficulty level appropriate?
    Detection task completed?
    Revenue loss estimate close?
  Calculate Precision, Recall, F1-score

NLP CHALLENGES FOR YOUR MODEL:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BASIC (Medium Difficulty):
  • Extract simple pricing from text ("Bank charges ₹500 per transaction")
  • Identify promotional periods with dates ("valid until Dec 31, 2025")
  • Detect missing escalation clauses (compare contract to catalogue)

INTERMEDIATE (High Difficulty):
  • Extract tiered pricing (volume-based, amount-based, frequency-based)
  • Identify conditional discounts ("if amount >₹50L, discount to ₹16000")
  • Detect capped escalation ("escalation capped at ₹0.50/year")
  • Parse multi-condition logic ("if >5 LCs per quarter AND contract >2 years")

ADVANCED (Very High Difficulty):
  • Extract relationship-tier based pricing matrix (Platinum/Gold/Standard)
  • Identify bundled service inclusions ("unlimited amendments included")
  • Detect multi-layer discounts (10% + 5% nested discounts)
  • Parse rebate structures with thresholds ("if total fees >₹50L, rebate 5%")
  • Handle ambiguous terms and implicit conditions
