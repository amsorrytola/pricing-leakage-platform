## XI. ESCALATION METHODOLOGY

### Annual Escalation Clause

All fees listed in this Schedule (except those expressly marked "N/A" or "One-time") shall be subject to **annual escalation of 3.5% per annum**, effective **January 1st of each calendar year**.

**Calculation Formula:**
```
New Fee (Year N) = Previous Fee (Year N-1) × (1 + Escalation Rate)
```

**Example:**
- Service: RTGS Processing
- Current Fee (2025): INR 50.00
- Escalation Rate: 3.5% p.a.
- New Fee (2026): INR 50.00 × 1.035 = INR 51.75

### Special Escalation Clauses

Where explicitly stated in contract Section 3.3 (Special Pricing Terms), certain fees may have **frozen escalation** for a specified period or **custom escalation rates** different from 3.5% p.a.
